CASE STUDY


innoBand App

Project Highlights

innoWear's Chandan Sethi and I have a bit of a history. He came to me for a variety of things - from modelling on the innoWear website to creating it and designing the app UI and UX. The result has been a blockbuster product with a contextual and innovative UI.

  • g

    CONTEXTUAL

    Each icon is placed over a body part to allow users to understand more about their activities

  • d

    CLEAN

    Myself and the brand had felt a need for white, minimalism, and clean lines from the beginning

  • m

    EXTENSIBLE

    Interfaces were designed for a variety of platforms, devices, and aspect ratios

  • f

    INTERACTIVE

    More than just graphs and icons, each click in the app has a purpose and can provide more information

  • k

    RESEARCHED

    With my background in personal training and nutrition, I was able to infuse the app with scientific backing

  • b

    CUSTOMISABLE

    We placed an emphasis on the onboarding process and settings area to ensure users could control everything

UI + UX Design

You'll notice each element tracked is placed over a body part to help users visualise how their activity is benefiting them. For further information, users can tap each icon for their 'personal best' results, and see historical graphs as well.
