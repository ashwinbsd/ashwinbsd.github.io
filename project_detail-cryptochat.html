CASE STUDY


CryptoChat App

Project Highlights

CryptoChat was an app project dreamt up by myself and my business partner Zohaib. The idea was to "emotionally encrypt" messages by using a question and answer combo only the sender and receiver could know about. Messages were locked with the answer to the question, and unlocked using a circular scramble of letters.

UI Highlights

Our goal was to conceptualise the idea, create a prototype, and see how users reacted before building the app itself. Overall, interactions were easily adopted and enjoyed, but conceptual understanding of the app seemed too slow to move to market.
